In each round, you' ll be in a room with 5-10 students. Usually, it' s 6 or 7. Depending on how many students are competing, the 2 to 10 highest scorers from the combined preliminary rounds in each division then compete in the final rounds. The four divisions are kept entirely separate-there is no crossover, including in the final rounds. Each round takes about 20-30 minutes to complete, including the finals. 关于决赛： 决赛由各组别预赛成绩最高的选手共同参加，题目数量固定，得分最高者或率先达标者获胜。决赛形式可能不同，但赛前会向选手说明规则和晋级人数。 官网原文：In the final round, the top students (the number can vary from tournament to tournament based on hnow many competed in the preliminaries) in all divisions all compete at once. Most of the time, a set number of questions will be played and whoever is in the lead after all questions have been read is the champion. Additionally, if a student reaches a stated number (which can likewise vary from event to event) of questions, they may also win outright before the end of the match. While the format of the finals can vary from tournament to tournament, it will always be made clear to the competing students how it will work in advance, and the number of students who make the finals will be made clear during the Opening Meeting of the History Bee before the preliminary rounds begin. 比赛形式： 学生通过抢答正确得分，三人答错则该题结束，第三个错者扣1分。 达到8分（hubert所在的Junior Varsity）后该轮结束。 越早达到目标分，奖励分越高（最多加7分）。 三轮总分最高的学生进入决赛。 官网原文：Students each have a buzzer and attempt to be the first student to ring in and answer correctly. Students may ring in at any point in the question - they are encouraged to interrupt the moderator to do so. After they ring in, they give their answer.If they are correct, they get a point. If incorrect, they cannot buzz again on the question. Three incorrect answers given will end the question, at which point the moderator reveals the answer. They do not normally lose a point if they are incorrect except if they are the third student to answer incorrectly before the end of the question, in which case, they do lose a point (so it is possible, conceivably, to have a negative score). If the question has been read to completion, three incorrect answers will still end the question, but no penalty will be assessed. Once a student has reached 8 points for Junior Varsity and Varsity , or 5 points for Elementary and Middle School, that student is done for that round and students receive bonus points based on how early they reach 5 or 8 points.
