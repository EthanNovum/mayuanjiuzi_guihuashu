SIP usually expects to place 300+ students in areas such as astrophysics, biology, computer science, physics, psychology,and many others. Research projects vary every year, but cover a wide range of topics. While many SIP interns are "rising seniors" (in the summer between junior and senior year), this is not arequirement. Past Research Projects for reference https://sip.ucsc.edu/past-research-projects-2/ All Subject including as: Anthropology, Applied Artificial Intelligence, Art Culture & Stem, Astronomy & Astrophysics, Biomolecular Engineering, Chemistry & Biochemistry, Computational Media, Computer Science/ Computer Engineering, Earth & Planetary Sciences, Ecology & Evolutionary Biology, Electrical Engineering, Environmental Studies, Latin American & Latino Studies, Linguistics, Molecular, Cell, & Developmental Biology,Ocean Sciences, Physics, Psychology, Sociology) Students will be selected based on their level of interest in the subject to which they are being matched for research project placement, motivation to learn new concepts, and capacity for analytical thinking.
